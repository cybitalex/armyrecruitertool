## Key Talking Points

- **Keep it brief** - Get to the demo quickly
- **Focus on benefits** - Not technical details
- **Show, don't tell** - Let the demo speak for itself
- **Be prepared** - Have the application ready and tested
- **Answer questions** - But defer detailed technical questions to after demo
